Body piercings

Piercings are often used to express culture or self expression through the variety, along with the different method of them.

A majority of body piercings have originated from ancient origins and different cultures. Piercings back then were more used for pure aesthetics because a lot of those culturals liked the look of the gold and silver. Body piercings have been part of human culture for thousands of years which serve various symbols through religion and aesthetic purposes. As time has went on the view of piercings has also changed — it is no longer seen as deviance or rebellion or some sort of symbol. They are more seen as a way to self‑express yourself further and show individuality.

As piercings have developed so have the methods and range of piercings

As we have been able to learn more about piercings throughout the years their methods and supplies have also advanced. As we have went from using tools like bone needles we have then begun to penetrate the skin as we then evolved to autoclaving with sterile supplies. As piercings have grown more they became more popular in the 1970's as we were able to gain access to piercing guns and hollow needles with all sterile equipment. Overall the piercing equipment has changed a lot over the years but that has also caused the expansion of types of piercings and a wider range for more methods.
